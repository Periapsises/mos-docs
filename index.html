About

What is Mos?

Mos, short for "Mosfet", was a company founded in 1969 (Mos Technology).
They invented the famous Mos 6502 processor, known for its use in vintage computer systems such as the Apple II and the Commodore PET.
It was at its time the least expensive microprocessor on the market.

This project aims to recreate the processor through emulation and hopefully bring Garry's Mod players a pleasant experience with assembly and the challenge of working with an 8-bit microprocessor.
